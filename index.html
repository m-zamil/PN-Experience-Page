STANDARD SUPPLIES VS. PREMIUM KITS

Our standard supplies are for group use. Your host will bring them and take them back at the end of the event.

Premium Kits are individual kits we'll mail to each guest for them to keep—perfect for virtual events. They contain everything you need for a Paint Nite, plus an extra canvas and a box for storing your painting that doubles as a tabletop easel.
